Section 01

What Happens When Someone Sends a Message

Every message goes through 7 steps. There are no scripts — each response is built fresh based on what Mira understands about this person right now.

1
Message Arrives
The person types or speaks. Voice input gets cleaned up automatically before anything else happens.
2
Read Between the Lines
One AI call analyzes everything at once — not just what they said, but what they meant, how they're feeling, and whether their story is shifting.
clarity confidence capacity constraint guess emotional tone contradictions relationship health
3
Separate Fact from Interpretation
What the person actually said always wins over what Mira thinks they meant. If someone says "I'm fine," that overrides a frustrated-sounding tone.
4
Decide What to Do Next
21 rules check the conversation state in priority order. Safety comes first (is the person overwhelmed? frustrated?), then diagnostic progress (explore, validate, diagnose).
Safety first Then explore, validate, or diagnose Always has a fallback
5
Build Mira's Instructions
A custom set of instructions is assembled for this exact moment — Mira's personality + what phase we're in + any emotional adjustments needed + the right conversation style for this person.
6
Generate the Response
Claude (Anthropic's AI) writes Mira's response using those custom instructions and the full conversation history.
7
Quality Check
Before sending, we verify the response actually does what it's supposed to. No accidentally pushing a sale during exploration. No diagnosing before asking permission. If it fails, we regenerate.
Section 02

Mira Remembers Everything

Three systems help Mira get smarter as the conversation progresses — she never goes in circles, never sounds repetitive, and tracks the person's growing awareness.

What We've Covered

Tracks every topic discussed and calculates how much ground we've covered. When we've explored enough territory, Mira knows it's time to move toward a diagnosis instead of asking more questions.

  • Identifies new topics each turn
  • After 8+ topics, starts moving toward answers
  • Won't revisit things already discussed
  • Progress score: 0–100%

Fresh Language

Mira rotates through different ways of acknowledging, reflecting, and asking questions. No two insight moments sound the same — and she limits deep reflections to 3 per conversation so they feel earned.

  • 6+ ways to open an insight
  • 5+ ways to check if something resonates
  • At least 3 turns between deep reflections
  • Tracks every phrase to avoid repeats

Growth Tracking

Follows the person's journey from surface-level answers to real self-understanding. Measures the quality of their insights, not just how many words they type.

  • Counts genuine insights (not just "yeah, totally")
  • Notes when they spot their own contradictions
  • Tracks expertise: newcomer to experienced
  • Self-awareness: starting out to deeply reflective
Section 03

Mira Reads the Room

She tracks the relationship in real time across four dimensions — and adjusts how she talks based on who this person is, not just what they said.

How Engaged Are They?
Highly engaged → Moderate → Low effort → Pushing back
Based on how much detail they share, whether they build on Mira's observations, or give one-word answers
How Much Do They Trust Mira?
Just starting → Growing → Solid ⤹ Broken
Trust only goes up — unless Mira misses the mark badly. Recovery requires real understanding, not just "sorry about that."
What Kind of Person Are They?
Open & reflective Straight to the point Testing the tool Working through feelings
A straight-to-the-point person gets concise answers. Someone working through feelings gets more space and validation.
Are They Getting Frustrated?
Not at all → A little → Clearly → Hostile
Each level gets a different response: gentle acknowledgment, a repair attempt, or a firm but respectful boundary.

Section 05

Built on Danny's Framework

Mira isn't just asking questions — she's applying Danny's diagnostic thinking in real time. Every principle he teaches is built into how she reasons.

Most people aren't confused — they're avoiding something they already suspect is true.
— Danny Iny, core principle built into Mira
One Bottleneck
Every business has one primary thing holding it back. Mira's job isn't to list 10 problems — it's to find the one that, once resolved, unlocks everything else.
The Lamppost Problem
People tend to work on familiar, comfortable problems while avoiding the harder real issue. Mira gently steers toward the uncomfortable truth.
Frozen Intent
Sometimes people genuinely want change but feel stuck — not because they don't trust the process, but because they've been disappointed too many times. Mira recognizes and names this.
What's Really Missing?
When someone isn't taking action, it's either motivation, ability, or a trigger that's missing. Mira figures out which one instead of assuming they just need a push.
Better Questions
"What would have to be true for that to be the right move?" Mira uses questions like these to cut through overthinking and surface what someone actually believes.
One Small Step
"You can't steer a parked car." For someone who isn't ready for coaching, Mira gives them one concrete thing to do — just enough to create forward motion.
